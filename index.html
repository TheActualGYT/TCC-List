Welcome to the official TCC List!

This is a collection of the hardest Geometry Dash levels beaten by the TCC, a community formed about 1.5 years ago by a group of 10 friends. The TCC started as a small group of players who shared a love for Geometry Dash and a passion to achieve any goals that came to their mind. Over time, the community has grown and continues to challenge each other to push their skills to the limit today. These levels represent the hard work, dedication, and resilience of the TCC members as they overcome some of the most difficult challenges they've ever experienced. This collection shows the achievements of the group and highlights their progress as players in the Geometry Dash community. Thanks for reading, enjoy the website! :D.
